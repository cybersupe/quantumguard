🔐 I just built QuantumGuard — an open-source quantum 
cryptography vulnerability scanner.

After completing my Master's in CS & Cybersecurity, 
I spent one night building a tool that solves a real problem:

Most enterprise codebases use RSA, ECC, and DH encryption.
Quantum computers will break all of these within this decade.
Companies need to migrate NOW — but don't know where to start.

QuantumGuard solves this by:
✅ Scanning your entire codebase automatically
✅ Detecting every quantum-vulnerable algorithm
✅ Giving your project a Quantum Readiness Score (0-100)
✅ Suggesting NIST-approved PQC replacements for every finding

Built with Python, tree-sitter, and Claude AI.

This is just the beginning — AI-powered reports and 
a web dashboard coming soon.
